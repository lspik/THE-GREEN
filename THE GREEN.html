TOP 6 PLANT'S FOR US

SPATHIPHYLLUM

DESCRIPTION: It is very elegant and beautiful, actively synthesizes oxygen and is able to create a feeling of freshness even in a confined space. Spathiphyllum kills mold and fungus spores, disinfects the air. Helps to reduce stress levels, relieves nervous tension.

CHLOROPHYTUM

DESCRIPTION: An unpretentious shade-tolerant flower grows quickly and does not require close attention. However, it effectively copes with air disinfection and removal of excess CO2. It actively absorbs mold spores, removes nitrogen oxides from the air where food is cooked on natural gas.

ALOE

DESCRIPTION: Agave is indispensable in an apartment where the floor is covered with linoleum. Modern building materials vaporize a lot of substances harmful to humans into the atmosphere. Aloe extracts special components that help neutralize the effect of such vapors.

AGLAONEMA

DESCRIPTION: At home in India and Malaysia, aglaonema is a flower of good luck. It is believed that the plant has positive energy and is able to share it with its owners. No less effectively filters the air from harmful compounds and saturates the atmosphere with negatively charged ions.

BEGONIA

DESCRIPTION: Lovers of indoor plants know that there are varieties of begonia that differ spectacular flowering, and there are decorative varieties with an unusual shape of leaves. Begonias are able to disinfect the air, its leaves reduce the amount of dust in the room.

ASPARAGUS

DESCRIPTION: It is known as the plant that gave the world the first studied amino acid - asparagine, which marked the beginning of a new milestone in the development of science. Asparagus is eaten, used as a medicinal plant and grown as an ornamental crop.
