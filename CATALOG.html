ALOE

DESCRIPTION: Agave is indispensable in an apartment where the floor is covered with linoleum. Modern building materials vaporize a lot of substances harmful to humans into the atmosphere. Aloe extracts special components that help neutralize the effect of such vapors.

AGLAONEMA

DESCRIPTION: At home in India and Malaysia, aglaonema is a flower of good luck. It is believed that the plant has positive energy and is able to share it with its owners. No less effectively filters the air from harmful compounds and saturates the atmosphere with negatively charged ions.

BEGONIA

DESCRIPTION: Lovers of indoor plants know that there are varieties of begonia that differ spectacular flowering, and there are decorative varieties with an unusual shape of leaves. Begonias are able to disinfect the air, its leaves reduce the amount of dust in the room.

ASPARAGUS

DESCRIPTION: It is known as the plant that gave the world the first studied amino acid - asparagine, which marked the beginning of a new milestone in the development of science. Asparagus is eaten, used as a medicinal plant and grown as an ornamental crop.

CHAMAEDOREA

DESCRIPTION: Chamaedorea is also called bamboo palm. It looks spectacular in the interior and also humidifies the air and cleans it from formaldehyde and benzene.Likes moderate light, cool, grows well at a temperature of +12 to +20 ° C. But sudden changes in temperature are not for it, so Chamaedorea should be protected from draughts.

FICUS

DESCRIPTION: Ficus is ascribed medicinal properties. In folk medicine it is used against inflammatory diseases. But here you should be careful: sometimes ficus causes allergies. For the same reason, it is placed so that pets cannot taste it.

BENJAMIN FICUS

DESCRIPTION: The optimum temperature for keeping the plant is 25-30 degrees in summer and 16-20 degrees in winter. Ficus, which have motley leaves and stand in the bedroom, needs good light. If the plant has green leaves, it will be comfortable in a room with diffused light and penumbra.

CRASSULA

DESCRIPTION: The fleshy leaves of the crassula can be green or silver in color, have a red border and a variety of shapes. High decorative value along with unpretentiousness make the Thistle one of the most popular houseplants.

BAMBOO

DESCRIPTION: More and more often in flower stores you can see interesting plants, the main difference of which shape is a zigzag or spiral. Beautiful shoots are usually decorated with small green leaves. The popular name of the plant is "happy bamboo". With bamboo growing in nature, it is associated only with the name and similar appearance.

PHALAENOPSIS ORCHID

DESCRIPTION: In nature, phalaenopsis grow in the Philippines, Australia and Southeast Asia. Most often they live on tree trunks, obtaining food from decomposed organic matter in branch forks and crevices of trunks, and moisture from the air.
